  • How can I avail a scholarship?

    • Availing a scholarship is a three stage process. The first step is to use the application form and provide a write up of yourself and the need for scholarship. Attaching the required documents and references is a must. The second stage is a verification stage where the member from the trust would be conducting a verification to validate the information. The member then pushes you into a pool of shortlisted applications. The third step, the member sends out scholarship invitations after an internal selection process to selected talents based on the academic scores, extracurricular talents and comparative merit of other students in the pool. One can drop and email or contact the trust for a detailed explanation.
